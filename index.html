Health

Strawberries are rich in vitamin C and other antioxidants, which help reduce the risk of serious health conditions like cancer, diabetes, stroke, and heart disease. But in order to gain all the benefits starawberries should be washed properly. here's how:

  1. Mix one teaspoon of baking soda with four cups of water and stir until dissolved
  2. dd the strawberries to the mixture and toss them with clean hands.
  3. Let the strawberries soak in the mixture for 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. Rinse the berries clean and then they're ready to eat.
